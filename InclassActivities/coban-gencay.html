About us

One of the pioneers of the third wave coffee movement, JavaJam Coffee House imports and roasts qualified JavaJam coffee beans itself.
And freshly served to its guests in all its branches. In this context, JavaJam Coffee House is suitable for the third wave coffee trend
and boutique service concept. uses high quality equipment and brewing tools.

Coffee House; with its boutique service approach, constantly developing and institutionalized structure, perspective that does not compromise on
quality and expert staff, It continues to operate on the basis of the satisfaction of its guests and thus continues to grow in line with its targets.

JavaJam Coffee House grows and develops in line with its commitment, respect and belief to the principles and values of the Republic of Turkey.
In this context, JavaJam Coffee House is aware of its debt to the Republic of Turkey and the people of the Republic of Turkey, which is the reason
for its existence, and takes the necessary steps to pay this debt. will continue to develop and throw, and to produce social responsibility projects.
